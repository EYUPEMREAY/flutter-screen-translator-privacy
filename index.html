Privacy Policy — Screen Translator

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Screen Translator ("the app") is a tool that captures the content shown on your device screen, recognizes text in it, and translates that text. This policy explains what the app does with your data. We have tried to keep the app's data footprint as small as possible.

Summary

The app does not have its own servers. It does not collect, sell, or store your personal information. Screen images are processed on your device and are never uploaded anywhere. Only the recognized text is sent, directly from your device, to Google's Cloud Translation service to be translated.

Screen capture

When you tap the floating bubble, the app captures the current screen using Android's MediaProjection system, but only after you have granted permission. The captured image is used solely to recognize text on your device using on-device optical character recognition (OCR). The screen image never leaves your device and is deleted from temporary storage immediately after text recognition. The app only captures the screen when you manually tap the bubble — it never captures automatically or in the background.

Text recognition (OCR)

Text recognition is performed entirely on your device using Google's ML Kit, which runs locally. Recognizing text does not require an internet connection and does not send any image or text off your device.

Translation

To translate the recognized text, the app sends that text — and only that text, never any image — directly from your device to the Google Cloud Translation API, using the API key that you provide and that is stored only on your device. This request goes straight to Google; it does not pass through any server operated by us. Google's handling of that text is governed by Google's own privacy policy and Cloud Translation terms. The app does not keep a history of your translations.

Data storage on your device

The app stores your settings (such as your chosen languages and your Google Cloud Translation API key) locally on your device using Android's secure storage. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party other than Google as described above. You can remove it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Data we do not collect

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no crash-reporting services. We do not collect device identifiers, location, contacts, or any other personal data.

Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
